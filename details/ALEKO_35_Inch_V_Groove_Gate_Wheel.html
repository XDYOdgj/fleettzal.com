ALEKO 3.5 Inch V-Groove Gate Wheel

Engineered for performance and reliability, the ALEKO 3.5 Inch V-Groove Gate Wheel is ideal for both industrial and commercial gate systems. Crafted from high-quality galvanized steel, it is built to last even under heavy use, while delivering smooth and quiet operation. The V-groove design ensures a perfect fit within V-profile sliding gate tracks, making it a durable and efficient choice for seamless gate movement.

Whether you're looking for a gate wheel that can handle the demands of an industrial setting or the everyday use of a residential gate, these wheels are designed with precision. Their 3.5-inch diameter and sturdy construction make them suitable for flat mounting under gates, supporting weights up to 800Lb (350kg) with ease. The no-maintenance feature sets them apart, offering long-lasting use without the need for frequent adjustments or greasing.
