Qikuver Shower Door Rollers

Upgrade your bathroom with durable and ultra-quiet shower door rollers designed to last for years. Perfect for glass sliding doors and shower enclosures.

Built to Last

Our Qikuver shower door rollers are crafted from a combination of copper, stainless steel, and nylon to ensure long-lasting performance, even with frequent use. The strong load-bearing capacity allows for smooth and effortless door movement, while wear-resistant materials help prevent rust and corrosion. This makes them an ideal replacement for bathroom glass doors, offering reliability and safety every time you use them.

Seamless Fit and Easy Installation

Designed for most shower enclosures, cabins, and steam cabin doors, these rollers are compatible with glass doors measuring 4-6mm in thickness. The 29mm size and 15-17mm installation aperture ensure a perfect fit and effortless setup. Enjoy quiet, stable movement that enhances both functionality and comfort in your bathroom space.
