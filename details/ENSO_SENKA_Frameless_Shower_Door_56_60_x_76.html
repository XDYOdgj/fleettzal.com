ENSO SENKA Frameless Shower Door

Elevate your bathroom's style with the sleek and modern design of the ENSO SENKA frameless shower door. This innovative product combines beauty with functionality, bringing a contemporary look to your space. Crafted from 5/16" (8mm) SGCC certified tempered glass, it ensures maximum safety and durability even with daily use, making it a reliable choice for your home.

The frameless design adds a minimalist touch to your bathroom, creating an open and luxurious feel. With all necessary installation components included, it's easy to set up, saving you time and hassle. A 2-year warranty guarantees peace of mind and long-term satisfaction with the product.

Featuring a matte black and gold finish, this shower door offers an elegant upgrade to your existing fixtures. The easy-to-clean coating on both sides of the glass ensures effortless maintenance, keeping your bathroom looking fresh without needing constant scrubbing. Whether you're looking for a left or right opening, the reversible design makes it adaptable to various bathroom layouts.

Equipped with a tight-fitting waterproof sealing strip, this door provides excellent splash protection, keeping the floor dry and preventing water from escaping outside the shower area. Its walk-in width of 26 2/5 inches makes it perfect for larger showers, offering comfort and space.

The robust stainless steel hardware ensures smooth and quiet operation, while also being resistant to corrosion and wear. This makes the ENSO SENKA frameless shower door a durable yet stylish option for any bathroom design.
