BOODVON's 4-inch Heavy Duty Gate Caster

BOODVON's 4-inch heavy duty gate caster offers an unbeatable combination of strength, comfort, and durability. Built to handle the toughest gate environments, these casters deliver smooth and quiet operation on flat and uneven surfaces alike. The spring-loaded mechanism automatically adjusts gate height, ensuring seamless movement and reducing stress on gate hardware and fence posts.

With 360-degree rotation, these gate casters make it easy to navigate gates in any direction, ideal for frequent use. The robust TPR material paired with sturdy galvanised steel brackets provides stability and impact resistance, while keeping the unit lightweight for effortless installation. Each caster is designed to support up to 220 lbs, making them perfect for heavy gate applications.

Versatile and Reliable Outdoor Gate Caster

Whether you're dealing with wooden, metal, vinyl, or PVC gates, BOODVON's gate casters are the perfect solution. They are engineered to perform flawlessly on a wide range of surfaces, including asphalt, brick, concrete, soil, grass, and gravel. The built-in shock-absorbing technology helps reduce noise and protects your gate from wear and tear over time.

Designed for long-term outdoor use, these casters are equipped with a high-grade white powder coating that resists rust, corrosion, and fading. Every gate caster comes with all the necessary hardware for hassle-free installation, making them a must-have for any home or business looking for reliable gate support.
